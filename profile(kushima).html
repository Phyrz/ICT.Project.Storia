Hey, I'm Sakura!

    To say that my experience as a junior high school student was something is an understatement. It was a rollercoaster ride of emotions, growth, learning, and more, in both good and bad. What made my years in junior high school more memorable are the friends I made. Even though I've fallen out with most of them during the pandemic, I still hold fond memories of our friendships. My teachers throughout the years have also made an impact in my junior high years.

    What I especially missed before the pandemic happened were the events I joined, dancing events, to be specific. Dancing was and still is my passion. The ability to execute my skills to a crowd was thrilling. I had always looked forward to learning and practicing choreography. If there was an occasion that dancing is involved, you could already count on me being there. It is such a shame that the latest event I joined, the year's theater performance, was cut short due to the pandemic. I haven't accomplished many. However, I had overcome a lot, particularly in my productivity and social activity. Although that had deteriorated since the pandemic, I still look forward to overcoming them once again nonetheless. Overall, I love my experience as a junior high student and would relive it any day. I am proud of who I am today, and I look forward to what the future has in store for me.
